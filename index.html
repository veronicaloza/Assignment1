Space in the Mind of a Machine : Immersive Narratives

Director and founder of Refik Anadol Studio in Los Angeles and researcher in the Department of Design Media Arts at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), Refik Anadol collaborates with machine intelligence to create multi-sensory immersive environments that reinvigorate the public realm, animate surroundings and explore the non-linearity of time. His works are colourful, thoughtful, experimental and engaging.
